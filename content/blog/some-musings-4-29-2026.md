Navigating the black pavement of the Burke-Gilman Trail, 
    
I blaze through the wind, trees and bushes breezing by

Listening to Dreamer by Laufey, I breathe and soak in 

the freshwater aroma of Lake Washington,

hearing distant seagull screeches in the background

As I enter the grounds of Gasworks Park, I head to 

the shore of the lake and absorb the magnificent Space Needle, 

Mount Rainier, and setting sun

My lungs and legs still burn, but my mind has gone quiet

Here where I stand, gazing into the horizon.
